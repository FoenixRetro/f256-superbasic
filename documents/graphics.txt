Graphics Module
---------------

Doesn't clip H/V.
Keeps track of the last two points.

Default bitmap $10000 (must be on 8k boundary)
Default GLUT 0
Default height 240

Graphics Command passed in AXY.

ccccccrx xxxxxxxx yyyyyyyy

cccccccc

1 COLOUR (X fgr, Y bgr)
2 CLEAR

8 MOVE 		
9 LINE 	
10 RECT
11 Fill RECT
12 ELLIPSE
13 Fill ELLIPSE 
14 DRAWC xxxxxxx character yyyyyyyy scale (from font memory),moves to next horizontal position (hflip/vflip option in upper bits of Y)
15 DRAWS sprite xxxxxxx yyyyyyy x:y forms a x 64 sprite address (hflip/vflip option)
